Implements the operators Flow and Connector to Spatial System Dynamics Extension

Types

Connector A Connector operation represents a target or source of energy, information or matter of a spatial region.
Flow A Flow operation represents continuous transference of energy between two spatial Connectors.
Timer A Timer is an event-based scheduler that runs the simulation.
